About FreePcGamesPortal:

FreePCGamesPortal started in 2012 for sharing PC games in compressed size for users who have slow internet or low computer specification. Many modern games very large and difficult to download, so we provide smaller build versions when possible.

All games on this website uploaded by our team and contributors from different places. Before upload we try test the game but some builds are experimental and may behave different on some computers.

This website is run by small group of PC game fans who want make downloading easier for everyone. Our goal is simple: free games, smaller size, and easy download for all users.
